About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I built this entire website from scratch using my self taught knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Last summer, my girlfriend and I started a blog about movies. When we were having trouble getting traffic to our site, we discovered the website builder we used was terrible for SEO. So we decided to build our own website from scratch. I took online tutorials, but quickly learned that I needed more if I was going to succeed in teaching myself how to code. Then I found Team Treehouse, a website dedicated to teaching beginners how to become web developers.

Since then, I have been studying HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through their online classes. I started out as a regular subscriber, but in January of 2018, I decided to enroll in the Front End Web Development Techdegree program. Below are examples of the projects I have been building. Click on the images to view the full site, and more projects are on the way!
